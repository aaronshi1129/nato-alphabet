What is the NATO Phonetic Alphabet?

The NATO phonetic alphabet is a spelling alphabet used by airline pilots, police, military, and others to communicate clearly over radio and telephone. Each letter is assigned a code word so that critical combinations of letters and numbers can be pronounced and understood despite language barriers and transmission static.
